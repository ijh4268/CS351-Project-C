CS351 - Project C

Instructions:

  1. Use the 'w', 'a', 's', and 'd' keys to move around the scene.
  2. Use the arrow keys to adjust the viewing angle of the camera.
  3. You can turn the 'reactor' (two torus' and a sphere) by clicking and dragging with your mouse.
  4. Press the Run/Stop button to toggle the animation.
  5. Use the "Spin <<" and "Spin >>" buttons to increase or decrease the rate of the animation.
  6. Look inside the tunnel to see some cool rotating stars!

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
